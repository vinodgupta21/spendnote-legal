Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 January 2026

SpendNote respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the SpendNote mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

SpendNote does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on external servers.

The app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Notes created by you
  • Expenses, bills, and related financial records
  • App preferences (theme, reminders, settings)

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. SpendNote does not upload or transmit your data to any remote server by default.

If backup or export features are enabled in future versions, you will be explicitly informed before any data leaves your device.

3. Permissions

SpendNote may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminders for expenses, bills, or notes.
  • Storage (optional): To export or back up your data when you choose to.

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and never for tracking or advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

SpendNote does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, since all data is stored on your device, you are responsible for securing your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

SpendNote is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
